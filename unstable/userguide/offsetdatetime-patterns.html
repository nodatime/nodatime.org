Custom Patterns

The custom format patterns for local date and time are the same as the custom patterns for LocalDateTime with one extra specifier for the offset.

The "o" specifier must always be followed by a pattern for Offset within angle brackets. The pattern may be a standard pattern or a custom pattern. For example, a pattern of "yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss o<G>" might produce output of "2013-07-17 06:20:35 Z" or "2013-07-17 07:20:35 +01:00".
